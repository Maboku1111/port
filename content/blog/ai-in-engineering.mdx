# The Role of AI in Engineering 🤖

Artificial Intelligence is **redefining** how engineers **design, test, and innovate**.

## 🔍 How AI is Revolutionizing Engineering
- **Predictive Maintenance** – AI prevents failures before they happen.
- **Generative Design** – Algorithms create optimized structures.
- **Autonomous Systems** – Robotics & automation streamline processes.

AI isn’t replacing engineers—it’s **enhancing** them.
